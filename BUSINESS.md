# Speak with Dreams — Business Summary

## What It Is

Speak with Dreams (branded "reverie") is a voice-first dream journaling PWA. Users record dreams by speaking — the app transcribes, titles, and stores them. Premium tiers unlock AI interpretation, pattern analysis, social circles, and a constellation map of all their dreams.

## Target Audience

**Primary**: Ages 18–35, spiritually curious, interested in self-discovery, psychology, and mindfulness. Users who already journal but find writing too slow in the morning. Heavy social media users who respond to story-like sharing formats.

**Secondary**: Therapy clients, lucid dreaming practitioners, people in personal development communities (Reddit r/dreams, TikTok spirituality, journaling communities).

**Distribution**: Organic via social sharing (dream stories), word-of-mouth in niche communities, WhatsApp integration for passive logging.

## Business Model

### Free Tier
- Record up to **3 dreams per day**
- Basic journal (title, mood, tags, transcript)
- Dream story (24h Instagram-style)
- Private by default with visibility controls

### Premium — €5.79/mo (€69.99/yr)
- Unlimited daily recordings
- Dream Constellation (3D star map of all dreams)
- Recurring pattern & symbol insights
- Dashboard analytics
- Dream export

### Premium + AI — €9.99/mo (€89.99/yr)
- Everything in Premium
- AI dream interpretation (5 frameworks: Jungian, Freudian, Symbolic, Narrative, Neuroscience)
- "Ask Your Dreams" AI chat (Q&A over your dream history)
- Auto-generated analysis after each recording
- Deeper pattern connections across dreams

### Friends Plan — €7.49/mo (€89.99/yr)
- Premium + AI for you + shareable dream circle
- Circle members can view each other's dreams
- Shared pattern analysis across circle

## Key Metrics to Track

- Day-1 / Day-7 retention (voice recording is high-friction — if they record once, they often come back)
- Free-to-paid conversion rate (triggered at 3 dreams/day wall)
- Daily Active Dreamers (DAD)
- Dreams recorded per user per week
- Story views / circle engagement

## Monetisation Triggers

1. **Daily limit wall** — shown after 3rd recording (high-intent moment)
2. **AI interpretation gate** — shown when user taps "Analyze deeper"
3. **Constellation locked** — teased in nav, requires premium
4. **Circle size limit** — free tier limited to 1 circle of 3 people

## Growth Levers

- **Dream stories** are public — shareable links drive organic installs
- **Circle invites** — users recruit friends directly from the app
- **WhatsApp bot** — zero-UI logging creates sticky daily habit
- **Zodiac/astrology angle** — hooks spirituality-adjacent audiences on TikTok/Instagram
